Fund us

All Eternal-Twin games are entirely free, we depend entirely on your donations for our financial sustainability. We do not seek profit, so the money donated to the project will be used to maintain the required servers and services. Please note that donations are voluntary and do not bring any in-game benefits.

For the sake of transparency, we have opted to use OpenCollective for donations and expenses management. OpenCollective is a reliable platform that lets you know exactly where your money is going.

Our current objective is 50 € per month. This amount will be split as follows : 20 € for servers, 15 € for email service, 10 € for backups, 5 to 10 € for domain names. The extra funds will be used to either provide better servers or saved for future expenses.
